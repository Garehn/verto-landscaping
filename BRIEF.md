# Verto Landscapes — Redesign Brief

## Purpose
Transform the existing editorial-but-static site into a cinematic, motion-rich
experience that signals a $10,000+ design commission. Reference: the "Meridian
Development Group" showcase site from polidori.dev's Instagram reel
(instagram.com/reels/DZl4PovRcYy) — dark cinematic full-bleed photography,
corner-anchored micro-typography, buttery smooth scroll, restrained luxury.

## Audience
Affluent Sydney homeowners (Mosman, Castlecrag, Wahroonga, Northern Beaches)
commissioning $100k+ gardens. They buy trust, taste and patience — not price.

## The page's single job
Make a visitor *feel* the studio's calibre within 5 seconds of landing, and get
them to send an enquiry to Rory.

## Differentiation
The motion and photography treatment ARE the differentiation. Every other Sydney
landscaper has a static template. This site should feel like a film title
sequence for a garden.

## Constraints
- Keep the existing copy voice ("Gardens that hold time", "Slow on purpose") —
  it is already right for the audience.
- Keep existing local photography (public/images/*).
- Next.js 15 App Router; deployed on Vercel.
- Reduced-motion users get a fully readable, calm site.
